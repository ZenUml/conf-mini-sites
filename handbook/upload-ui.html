In-product experienceUpload UI — uploading & preview

The in-product surface a Confluence author sees inside the Connect macro-editor iframe: a multi-file bundle streaming up with checks ticking green, then the published mini-site rendered live and embedded. Use the in-prototype switcher to move between Uploading and Preview.

✨
Produced by the fireworks-design workflow — 8 aesthetic directions explored in parallel, scored by a 6-dimension judge panel, synthesized, then adversarially refined over 3 rounds (65 agents). This is the synthesized winner; the 8 explored drafts live alongside it in design/upload-ui/.

The winning direction — Bold Editorial

Magazine-grade hierarchy: a high-contrast serif display face (Fraunces) carries the emotional beats — a 92px overall-percentage numeral as the upload hero and an “It’s live.” headline at the reveal — while every functional control stays clean native-Atlassian sans (Inter) with a JetBrains Mono file manifest. Editorial drama lives in typography and rhythm, never in decorating the tool.

Panel ranking (avg of 6 dimensions)

  1. Bold Editorial — 8.0 ✓ winner
  2. Swiss Minimal — 7.67 · Dark Premium — 7.67 · Glass/Aurora — 7.67
  3. Soft Organic — 7.5 · Mono Tech — 7.5 · Neo-Brutalist — 7.33 · Vibrant Gradient — 7.17
